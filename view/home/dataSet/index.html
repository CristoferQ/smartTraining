Examples and Attributes

Information and detail for attributes in data set of mutations reported for pVHL.

Examples in DataSet

256 examples were considered to train and develop classification models associated with the detection of clinical relevance that present mutations reported in von Hippel-Lindau, Equally distributed among clinically relevant mutations and not.

Attributes considerated

15 were the attributes considered for the training of the classification models, which are related to the reported mutation, phylogenetic and structural information, topological knowledge of interaction sectors and clinical relevance.

Superfice Sector

Topological information associated with the protein-protein interaction sectors is relevant when inducing the partitions associated with the development of models using the MLSMPc methodology for the generation of classifiers.

Protein-Protein interaction sector

pVHL has 13 sectors of protein-protein interaction, in each of which mutations associated with von Hippel-Lindau have been reported. Each site presents a different number of reports. This difference is mainly due to the conservation of the waste in said sites. A distribution of the number of mutations by sector is observed below.
