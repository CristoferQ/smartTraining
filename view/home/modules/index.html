Results of process

Results obtained in the training of models.

Interaction protein-protein Sector

The use of topological information of the protein associated with the protein-protein interaction sectors allowed the generation of 13 meta-classifiers using the MLSMPc methodology, divisions that increases the performance of the classifiers.

Training step

All the models were trained using the MLSMPc methodology, which includes training 1200 models for each interaction sector and selecting the most representative of the sample based on statistical techniques.

Algorithms in classifiers

The algorithms associated with the models vary between Naive Bayes, SVM, KNN and Random Forest. All of them were trained through a training distribution associated with multiple validations with cross validation, selecting the most stable models.
